Eco Tips for Improving Air Quality

1. Use Public Transportation

Reduce your carbon footprint by opting for public transportation, carpooling, or cycling instead of driving alone.

2. Conserve Energy

Save energy by turning off lights and unplugging devices when not in use, which can help reduce air pollutants from power plants.

3. Plant Trees

Trees absorb pollutants and produce oxygen. Planting trees around your home and community can improve air quality.

4. Avoid Burning Trash

Burning waste releases harmful pollutants. Dispose of waste properly and consider recycling or composting.

5. Maintain Your Vehicle

Ensure your vehicle is in good condition to prevent excessive emissions, which contribute to air pollution.
