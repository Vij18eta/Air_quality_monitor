About Us

Welcome to Ambiance

Ambiance is a revolutionary website dedicated to collecting and providing air quality information from satellites. Our user-friendly platform allows you to access real-time air quality index (AQI) maps, ensuring that you stay informed about the air you breathe.

Our Features

  • Air Quality Heat Map

    Visualize air quality data through our interactive heat map, enabling you to see pollution levels in real time.

  • Your Thoughts, Our Actions..!!

    Share your suggestions and feedback with us. We value your input and strive to improve our services based on your ideas.

  • Breathify

    Engage in our eco-gamification feature that motivates you to maintain streaks while exploring ways to reduce pollution. Earn rewards as you contribute to a healthier environment!

Historical Data

Our platform also offers historical air quality index information for different cities, allowing users to track changes and trends over time.

Eco Tips

We provide actionable tips on reducing pollution and enhancing air quality. Join us in making a difference!
